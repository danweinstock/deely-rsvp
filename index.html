First Date

In 2011, a certain social worker messaged a handsome man on JDate. Her profile peaked his interest with her love of slap-stick movies and his curiosity about what the heck a "harm-reductionist vegetarian" was. She was drawn in by his knowledge of random facts, his sweetness and great smile. After a month of waiting (while Neely took forever to move into her new place), it was finally time for their first date. They were excited to meet. They went to Snakes and Lattes, had fantastic hot chocolate, played some board games and talked until late. Towards the end of the date, Neely decided to cut to the chase and ask Dan if he wanted to go on another date and he said yes. That was the beginning.

The Last 5 Years (yes we know it's been 5 years)

Over the last 5 years they have had fun and lots of laughs together, whether it's singing and dancing to musicals around the apartment, cooking, their annual bike rides that go awry, watching fireworks from their balcony on any given national holiday, roadtrips out East and West or just chilling on the couch watching their latest series.

The Proposal - August 28, 2016

Neely, the dog lover, suggested that it would be cute that when Dan proposed he do so with a puppy. Dan, being the practical guy that he is, went for the compromise and had a custom stuffed animal made to look just like her childhood dog, Kody. During one of their relaxing Saturday mornings, while watching "The Wedding Planner" for what felt to Dan like the 20th time, Dan felt that the time was right. He brought the "dog" down with the ring tied around it's neck and presented her "puppy" while on one knee. Even though she knew it was coming she still cried, as she does, and said yes!
